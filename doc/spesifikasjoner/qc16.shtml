QC1-6: Probable consistency - climatological relationship between parameters


The numbering is based on the following succession of meteorological elements:

Temperature (211-230), temperature/weather (231-270), wind/weather/waves/state of the sea (271-300), precipitation/snow/state of the ground (301-310), visibility/weather codes (311-320) and cloud/weather codes (321-330).

The information is considered to be suspect. Climatological inconsistency is flagged whenever an expression in the table below is satisfied.
